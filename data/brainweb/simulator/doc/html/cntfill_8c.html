Draws a polygon or polyline (depending on the closed parameter) on an image out of a CNT_USHORT with a given origin.

Parameters
contourPointer to source contour.
oxOffset along the X axis.
oyOffset along the Y axis.
imagePointer to destination image.
codeType of drawing : 0 means direct drawing, 1 means AND, 2 for OR and 3 for XOR (refer to IdImaDrawLine).
closed0 for a polyline, 1 for a polygone (closes the polyline by drawing an additional line starting at last point of contour and ending at first point)
greylevelGrey level of the line to draw.
Returns
0 on failure, 1 on success.
